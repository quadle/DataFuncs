A Little About Me....

Hello there! My name is Kit Wesselhoeft and I'm a data engineer with 6 years of experience and a degree in computer science and statistics. I kick-started my academic adventure at Evergreen State College, where I initially harbored a passion for organic farming. But as fate would have it, I stumbled upon the captivating world of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) during a geography class that required us to analyze farmland using ARC maps. This newfound interest took me by storm, and overnight, I was determined to unravel the intricacies of this remarkable tool. Fast forward four years, and I proudly graduated, armed with a degree, multiple Arduino robots, and a wealth of knowledge.

After earning my degree, I embarked on a rewarding journey with a startup, where I was the third employee. Initially, my role involved developing simple cleaning scripts using R. However, the dynamic startup environment presented numerous opportunities, and within the first month, I orchestrated the transition from R to Python. This shift empowered our team to make full use of Azure and its array of serverless functions. It's been an incredible five-year journey with this company, during which I honed my skills in Data Engineering and business-oriented development.
